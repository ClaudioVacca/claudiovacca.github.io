Hi, I'm Claudio Vacca

Game Developer

Growing up with an incredible passion for video games, I quickly realized that I couldn't just stop at playing them. I've always had a burning desire to create games and give people the same experiences that made me fall in love with this medium. That's why I chose to become a programmer, a way like any other to bring these much-coveted experiences to life.

While studying for my degree in computer science, I am grateful to have been able to live the classic dream of an independent developer: a few guys who managed to create a demo from their bedroom that convinced a publisher to finance us. That's where the story of my first big game, Voodoo, officially began, paving the way for many other opportunities and leading me (after a few minor detours) to work on a large number of games and similar projects.

Unity and C# are my main tools and the ones I am most skilled at, but I am always ready to learn and master new ones.
My strengths are versatility, problem solving, and a great team spirit.
